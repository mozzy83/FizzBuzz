FizzBuzz App

When it counts, you FizzBuzz. A Javascript coding exercise using loops to print the numbers 1 to 100. For multiples of 3, or the Fizz number, display "Fizz". For multiples of 5, or the Buzz number, display "Buzz". For multiples of both display "FizzBuzz".

Instructions

Enter Fizz and Buzz numbers and click the Submit button to display your results.
